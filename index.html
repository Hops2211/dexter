Dexter Church Of Christ

Welcome

The Dexter Church of Christ is a family of believers that love God and one another. We are Christians seeking to honor God as we worship and praise Him and serve Him in our daily lives. We strive to make a difference in the lives of our members, their families and friends, in our community and throughout the world. We are a Church of Christ that meets in Dexter, MI. We are honored to have you as our guest. Please click on the menus above to get directions and learn more about us. If you have any questions or comments, please contact us.
